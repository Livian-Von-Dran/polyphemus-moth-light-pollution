The tiff and tpk files contain the entire floating point dataset from the 2015 World Atlas of Artificial Night Sky Brightness. The data represent the artificial component of the night sky brightness (natural sky brightness is not included). More details about the data are available from the data access webpage: http://doi.org/10.5880/GFZ.1.4.2016.001
